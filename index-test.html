Our Team

Dawud Rahman
Founder

Contrary to popular belief, Dawud has a can-do attitude to suit complex business requirements selecting the best mix of people, systems, tools and business processes. With over 15 years experience, he has successfully designed and delivered strategic enterprise information systems totalling £3.2 million of IT capital expenditure for fortune 500 clients across financial services, government, retail, media and telecoms sectors.
Dawud holds a Computer Science Bsc from King's College, London.

Gordon Marsh
Co-founder

Gordon has been a software quality professional for over 15 years and has delivered analytics platforms for major high street retailers. He has developed a passion for efficiency and excellence in the software engineering process. Gordon progressed to strategic quality assurance roles where he influenced not only QA teams but every stakeholder in the process to create better products.
Gordon holds a Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Bsc from Sussex University and an MBA from Imperial College.

Ashiyana Rahman
Web Designer

Ashiyana has influenced information strategy for local governments with her in depth legislative compliance knowledge of DDA and international usability guideline. Ashiyana holds a English Literature Bsc from Karnataka State University, Bangalore, India.
